Why you should care about adaption.

Each year, in California, it is estimated that 100,000 animals are euthanized. (Harvin 2020) Governor Newsom has decided to move the state of California as a “no-kill” state within the next 5 years. The number of animals who are in shelters and end up being euthanized could be dramatically decreased if more people would be more willing to adopt pets and welcome them into their home rather than buying them from a private party.

Top Reasons To Adopt A Pet!

You'll Be A Hero!

Each year, it's estimated that more than one million adoptable dogs and cats are euthanized in the United States.

You'll Get A Great Pet!

Most shelter pets wound up there because of a human problem like a move or a divorce, not because the animals did anything wrong.

It Will Cost You Less!

Usually when you adopt pets, the cost of spay/neuter, first vaccinations (and sometimes even microchipping!) is included in the adoption price, which can save you some of the upfront costs of adding a new member to your family.

Adoption Helps More Than One Animal

Overburdened shelters take in millions of stray, abused and lost animals every year, and by adopting an animal, you’re making room for others.
